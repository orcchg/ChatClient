Privacy Policy Last Modified: August 29, 2016 ChatClient is a lightweight and easy-to-use public chat. ---------- Information We Collect In order to use ChatClient, it requires you to set up a basic account, so we need to collect the following things: a unique username (login) of your choice, your email address and password. Any other user of ChatClient is able to see your login and email, but not your password. ----------- How We Use Information We collect this data and store it on our web server, and also share your login and email to any other user of ChatClient in order to personalize you among the others. ----------- How Long We Keep Your Data ChatClient keeps your login, email and password until you send us an email asking to completely remove this data from the database of our server.
